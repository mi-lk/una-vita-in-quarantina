This is your home.

In your home lives your body.
In your home lives your heart and soul.
In your home you stay... well until you - and everybody else - knows it's okay now... okay to go outside.

In time you'll make it. We all will.
Keep telling yourself the good things you didn't have the time to tell yourself before.
Keep yourself safe and sound.
Keep yourself at home where you're warm, where you may or may not be with friends and family, where you can keep your loved ones' minds at peace.

No, don't hurt yourself, amore. Stay strong. Learn from this experience.

Rest. Build yourself. Reach new heights. All within the safety of your loving vessel, within the walls that protect you.

So... what can one do if one lives their life in quarantine?
